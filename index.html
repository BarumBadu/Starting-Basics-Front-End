Images and About Specify Size

Pixels (px)

Pixels are a fixed unit of measurement. One pixel is equal to one dot on the computer screen.

Percentages (%)

Percentages are relative to the size of the parent element.

Ems (em)

An em is equal to the computed font-size of the element itself.

Viewport width (vw)

is equal to 1% of the viewport width.

Viewport height (vh)

is equal to 1% of the viewport height.

Smaller Dimension of the viewport (vmin)

is equal to 1% of the smaller dimension of the viewport (width or height).

Larger Dimension of the viewport (vmax)

is equal to 1% of the larger dimension of the viewport (width or height).

Rems (rem)

An em unit relative to the root element (typically the element), not the parent element.

Absolute Length Units (in, cm, mm, pt, pc)

These units represent physical units of measurement. However, their actual size may vary depending on the output medium (screen, print, etc.).

Auto

Some properties, like width or height, can be set to "auto" to allow the browser to calculate the size automatically based on content or other factors.
